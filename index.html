At MotoSavant, we offer you the flexibility to choose a drop-off location different from the pick-up branch. This convenient option comes in handy when it suits your specific travel plans. We want to make your bike rental experience as smooth as possible. To ensure a seamless process, our website displays available bikes based on their availability at each branch and the period you require the rental. This way, you can avoid any unnecessary conflicts or disappointment due to unavailability. When you find the perfect bike available on your desired dates, you have the option to proceed with the reservation. By paying a reservation fee, you secure the selected bike for your chosen dates. This means you can rest assured that your desired bike will be waiting for you when you arrive.
